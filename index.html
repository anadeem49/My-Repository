    What is computer science?



    It is the study of computer systems which
    entails software and its systems, including
    development, theory, design, & application



    What's most important about computer science is
    that it stresses an essential life skill: problem-solving.
    Professionals' use of creativity to yield efficient algorithms
    also stems from the need to brainstorm algorithmic best and
    worst case scenarios.
