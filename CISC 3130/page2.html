CS in Today's Workplace


Computer science holds precedence in virtually all aspects of today's workforce from finance to politics to public health

  • Employment of software developers is projected to grow 21 percent from 2018 to 2028, much faster than the average for all occupations.
  • Employment of applications developers is projected to grow 26 percent, and employment of systems developers is projected to grow 10 percent.

    • It goes to show that even introductory knowledge of programming languages like Java, C++ or Python can give an employee an advantage in his/her field
